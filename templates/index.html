    Frequently Asked Questions

    We understand that visiting a healthcare facility often comes with many questions. To help you feel informed and comfortable, we've answered some of the most frequently asked questions about our services, appointments, and policies.

    What are your clinic hours?

    Our clinic is open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM and on Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

    Do I need an appointment, or you accept walk-ins?

    While we encourage scheduling an appointment to reduce wait times, we do accept walk-ins based on availability. Priority is given to patients with appointments.

    What Insurance plans do you accept?

    We accept most major health insurance providers. Please contact us to confirm if your specific insurance plan is accepted.

    Are there any services provided for free?

    Yes, we offer free services such as basic health consultations, blood pressure checks, and health education sessions. See our pricing section for more details.

    How do I get my test results?

    Your test results will be available within a few days. You can receive them via phone, email, or by visiting our center. For certain tests, results may take longer, and our staff will inform you of any delays.

    What should I bring to my first appointment?

    For your first appointment, please bring a valid ID, any insurance information, a list of current medications, and any previous medical records if available. This helps us provide the best possible care tailored to your health history.
